I am looking to start over again.⌗

A lot of the time when I spend time trying to organize projects, I struggle. I strive in environments preconfigured for specificity, and a lot of the time it’s easy to figure out what you’re looking for when you have a roadmap. Most of life feels like a fine balance between having a map, and watching it deteriorate from overuse. I realize that at this point, I have spent way too long not investing time into any of my other interests outside of work. I have wanted to read Nietzsche and these other writers, but don’t feel like I have found the right spot or approach. It’s been a long time since I have taken notes, and as I get older I have a massive collection of writings that I need to get through. This is my space to coherently and simply organize these things, without having to rely on managing toolsets and builds more than I want to for what might never pan out to be a meaningful github project.

Part of my two project’s I am looking to finish on this blog within the next few months is taking notes on the Republic and translating https://www.vrin.fr/livre/9782711613649/gilles-deleuze. I have yet to find an english copy, and at one point was really interested in what Deleuze saw in Bergson. I am not looking to have a good translation of the text either, just something that is better than nothing.
